## How does the installment calculator work?

The installment calculator helps you determine your loan or installment monthly payments and total costs. Enter the total amount of the loan or product, annual interest rate and payment period into the calculator. The calculator calculates the monthly payment using the annuity loan formula:

**Monthly payment = (total amount * monthly interest rate) / (1 - (1 + monthly interest rate)^-months).**

Once the monthly payment is calculated, the calculator also tells you the total costs of the loan or installment and the amount of interest. This helps you understand how much you pay for the loan or installment in total and how much of it is interest.

The installment calculator is a useful tool for everyone who wants to make smart financing decisions. You can use it for smaller purchases as well as larger loans. This helps you manage your finances better and make more informed decisions.

Use the installment calculator and easily find out how much you pay monthly and how much interest accrues. This helps you plan your financing more efficiently and understand the impact of the loan or installment on your budget.
