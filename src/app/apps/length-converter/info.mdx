### Common conversions

- **1 foot = 30.48 cm** – Feet are common especially in the United States and Britain
- **1 inch = 2.54 cm** – Used for TV screen sizes
- **1 mile = 1.609 km** – Distance unit used in the United States
- **1 km = 0.621 miles** – Mnemonic: multiply by 0.6

### Uses

- **Travel** – Convert American miles to kilometers or feet to meters
- **TVs and screens** – 65 inch TV is about 165 cm wide
- **Height** – 6 feet (6 ft) is about 182.9 cm
- **Construction** – Convert inch dimensions to metric system
