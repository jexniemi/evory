## World Capitals Quiz

The World Capitals Quiz is a fast geography challenge where you see a capital city and pick the correct country. It’s designed for quick rounds, replayability, and memory training.

### What makes this quiz engaging

- Four-choice format with instant feedback
- Streak and level system for momentum
- Covers major capitals from multiple regions
- Great for students, travelers, and trivia players

### How it works

Each round shows one capital city. Your job is to identify the matching country from four options. Correct answers increase your score and streak. Mistakes cost lives.
