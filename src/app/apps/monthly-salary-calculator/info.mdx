## How to calculate monthly salary from hourly wage

Monthly salary is calculated by multiplying hourly wage by monthly working hours:

**Monthly salary = Hourly wage × Weekly hours × 52 ÷ 12**

For example, with an hourly wage of $18/hr and 37.5 h weekly working hours, the monthly salary is approximately **$2,925/month**.

Monthly working hours are obtained by the formula: weekly hours × 52 weeks ÷ 12 months.

### When is converting hourly wage to monthly salary useful?

- **Job change** — hourly paid work changes to monthly paid, and you want to compare income level
- **Loan application** — banks usually ask for monthly incomes, not hourly wage
- **Budgeting** — monthly salary is an easier starting point for planning expenses
- **Part-time work** — find out how much you earn per month with a smaller number of hours

### Gross or net?

The calculator works with both gross and net salary. Employment contracts and job advertisements usually state gross salary. If you want to know the take-home amount, use net salary.
