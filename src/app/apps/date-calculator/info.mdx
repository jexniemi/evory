## Date Calculator – Calculating Days, Weeks, and Months

The date calculator is a useful tool when you want to find out the time between two dates or calculate the date of an event. The calculator shows the difference in days, weeks, and months.

### When is the date calculator useful?

- **Project scheduling** – Calculate how many days or weeks between project start and end dates
- **Birthdays and anniversaries** – How many days until birthday or anniversary?
- **Pregnancy calculation** – How many weeks from the estimated date?
- **Contracts and deadlines** – When does a 30 or 90-day deadline expire?
- **Travel** – How long is your visa valid or how long does the trip last?

### Adding Days to a Date

With the calculator, you can also add or subtract days from any date. Enter the start date and the desired number of days, and the calculator will give you the exact result with the day of the week.

### How does the calculation work?

The difference between dates is calculated in milliseconds and converted to days by dividing by 86,400,000 (milliseconds in a day). Weeks are obtained by dividing days by 7 and months by dividing days by 30.44 (average month length).
