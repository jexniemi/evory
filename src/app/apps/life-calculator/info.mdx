## Life Calculator – How Many Days Have You Lived?

The life calculator computes the exact number of days, weeks, months, and hours you have been alive — from your birthday to this very moment. It also estimates your total heartbeats, breaths, and how long until your next birthday.

### What Does the Calculator Show?

Enter your date of birth and instantly see:

- **Total days lived** — Every single day since you were born
- **Total weeks and months** — A different perspective on your life's duration
- **Total hours and minutes** — The sheer scale of time you have experienced
- **Estimated heartbeats** — Based on average resting heart rate
- **Estimated breaths** — Based on average respiratory rate
- **Days until your next birthday** — A countdown to your celebration

### How You Spend Your Life (Average American)

Over an average 78-year lifespan, researchers estimate that Americans spend approximately:

- **Sleeping** — 26 years (33%)
- **Working** — 13 years (17%)
- **Screen time (TV, phone, computer)** — 11 years (14%)
- **Eating** — 4 years (5%)
- **Commuting** — 1.3 years (1.7%)
- **Doing housework** — 1.5 years (2%)
- **Exercising** — 1.3 years (1.7%)
- **Socializing in person** — 1 year (1.3%)

### Ideas for Using the Life Calculator

- **Celebrate unusual milestones** — Your 10,000th or 20,000th day of life is a unique occasion worth marking.
- **Share on social media** — "Today I have been alive for exactly 10,000 days!" makes for a great post.
- **Perspective and motivation** — Seeing the finite number of days you have lived (and approximately have remaining) can motivate you to make the most of each one.
- **Goal setting** — Set goals based on day counts rather than calendar dates for a fresh perspective on time.
