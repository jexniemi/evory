## Life Day Calculator – How many days have you lived?

The life day calculator calculates the exact number of lived days, weeks, months and hours from your birthday to this moment. In addition, you see estimates of the total number of heartbeats and breaths and how long until your next birthday.

### What can this be used for?

- **Inspiration** – You notice concretely how long a journey is behind
- **Celebration** – When to celebrate the 10,000th life day? (about 27 years old)
- **Sharing on social media** – Fun to share on birthday or anniversary
- **Goal setting** – Use lived days as a source of motivation

### Fun fact: 10,000 days celebration

The 10,000th day of life is reached at about 27 years and 4 months of age. Many celebrate this special milestone!

### Heartbeats and breaths

- **Heartbeat** – About 70 times per minute at rest (about 100,000 times per day)
- **Breathing** – About 15 times per minute (about 21,000 times per day)

These are statistical averages and vary depending on the person's age, fitness and health.

### Next birthday

The calculator calculates the exact date and number of days until your next birthday.
