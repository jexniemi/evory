## Cat Age Calculator — Convert Cat Years to Human Years

Cats age differently than humans, and the old "multiply by 7" rule is inaccurate. According to the American Association of Feline Practitioners (AAFP) and the American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA), a cat's first year is roughly equivalent to 15 human years, the second year adds about 9 more, and each subsequent year equals approximately 4 human years.

This calculator uses the veterinary-standard conversion method to give you an accurate human-equivalent age for your cat.

## How Cat-to-Human Age Conversion Works

Cats mature extremely fast in their first two years. A 1-year-old cat is already a teenager in human terms, and a 2-year-old cat is a young adult around age 24. After that, each cat year adds about 4 human years:

> **Year 1** ≈ 15 human years  
> **Year 2** ≈ +9 human years (total: 24)  
> **Year 3+** ≈ +4 human years per cat year

**Example:** A 7-year-old cat:
- First 2 years = 24 human years
- Next 5 years × 4 = +20 human years
- Total = **44 human years**

## Indoor vs Outdoor Cat Lifespan

Indoor cats live significantly longer than outdoor cats on average. Indoor cats typically live 12–18 years, while outdoor cats average only 2–5 years due to traffic, predators, diseases, and environmental hazards. Many indoor cats live well into their 20s with proper care.

## How to Help Your Cat Live Longer

- **Regular vet checkups**: Annual visits for cats under 7, twice yearly for seniors. Catch health issues early.
- **Quality nutrition**: Feed age-appropriate, high-protein food. Avoid overfeeding — obesity shortens cat lifespans significantly.
- **Hydration**: Provide fresh water daily. Consider a cat water fountain to encourage drinking.
- **Dental care**: Dental disease is common in cats over 3. Regular cleaning prevents pain and infection.
- **Mental stimulation**: Interactive toys, scratching posts, and window perches prevent boredom and cognitive decline.
- **Keep indoors**: Indoor cats face dramatically fewer risks and live 3–5 times longer on average.
