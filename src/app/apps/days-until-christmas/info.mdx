## How is Christmas celebrated?

Christmas is full of traditions and atmosphere. Here are some ways people usually celebrate Christmas:

- **Christmas foods**: Christmas dinner is an important part of Christmas. Typical Christmas foods include ham, casseroles, salads, and various Christmas pastries like gingerbread and cakes.

- **Christmas church**: Many people attend Christmas church on Christmas Eve. In church, you can hear the Christmas gospel reading and enjoy Christmas songs.

- **Santa Claus**: Santa Claus is believed in, and in many families, Santa visits on Christmas Eve bringing gifts to children.

- **Christmas gifts**: Giving and receiving gifts is an important part of Christmas. Most families exchange gifts on Christmas Eve.

- **Christmas sermons and traditions**: On Christmas Eve and Christmas Day, it is customary to sit together with family, enjoy good food, and spend time together. Many families also share Christmas sermons and other Christmas traditions, such as singing Christmas songs or reading Christmas stories.

In addition to these traditions, Christmas is celebrated with loved ones, and the atmosphere is warm and cozy. Christmas lighting and decoration are also important parts of creating the festive atmosphere.
