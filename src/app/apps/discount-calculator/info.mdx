## How does the discount calculator work?

The discount calculator helps you find out how much money you save with a discount. Enter the original price and discount percentage into the calculator. The calculator calculates the discounted price using the formula:

**Discounted price = original price * (1 - discount percentage / 100)**

Once the discounted price is calculated, the calculator also tells you the amount you save. This is calculated using the formula:

**Savings = original price * (discount percentage / 100)**

This way, you get an accurate picture of how much the discount affects the price and how much money you save.

The discount calculator is a useful tool for anyone who wants to make smart purchases and save money. You can use it for everyday shopping as well as larger purchases. This helps you manage your finances better and make more informed decisions.

Use the discount calculator and easily find out how much money you can save. This helps you plan your purchases more efficiently and take advantage of the best deals.
