## How to Calculate Annual Salary

Annual Salary from Monthly Salary:

**Annual Salary = Monthly Salary × 12 + Holiday Pay + Bonuses**

Annual Salary from Hourly Wage:

**Annual Salary = Hourly Wage × Weekly Hours × 52 ÷ 12 × (12 + Holiday Pay Months)**

For example, with a monthly salary of $3,200 including holiday pay, the annual salary is approximately **$40,000**.

### Vacation Pay

Vacation pay is typically a percentage of monthly salary, often around 50%. It is usually paid in connection with summer vacation. Vacation pay may be statutory or based on employment agreements. If your agreement does not include vacation pay, select "No" in the calculator.

### What Does Annual Salary Include?

Annual salary usually means gross salary for the whole year. It may include:

- 12 months' basic salary
- Holiday pay (usually 0.5 months)
- Performance bonuses and bonuses
- Benefits in kind (phone, car, housing benefits) are included in taxation

Overtime compensations are not usually included in annual salary because they vary monthly.

### When is Knowing Annual Salary Useful?

- **Taxation** — the income limit of the tax card is based on estimated annual income
- **Loan Application** — banks assess creditworthiness based on annual income
- **Salary Comparison** — salaries in different countries are often compared on an annual basis
- **Job Search** — some job postings state salary on an annual basis
