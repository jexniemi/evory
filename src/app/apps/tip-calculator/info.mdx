## How Does the Tip Calculator Work?

The tip calculator helps you calculate the exact tip amount and split it evenly among all payers. Enter the bill amount, tip percentage, and number of people splitting the bill. The calculator computes the tip using the formula:

**Tip = bill amount × (tip percentage / 100)**

Once the tip is calculated, it is added to the bill to get the total:

**Total = bill amount + tip**

The calculator then divides the total evenly among all payers.

Use the tip calculator to easily find out how much to tip and how much each person owes — it helps you plan your spending and make fair payments in a group.

## How Much Should You Tip?

Tipping customs vary widely around the world.

In the **United States**, it's standard to leave **15–20%** at restaurants. Tipping is also expected for hotel housekeeping, taxi drivers, and food delivery — typically $2–$5 per service.

In **Europe**, customs vary by country. In some places the tip is included in the bill; in others 10–15% is appreciated. In **Japan**, tipping can be considered rude. In **Southeast Asia**, a small tip is polite.

Quick reference:
- **Restaurant (US):** 15–20%
- **Bar / café:** $1–$2 per drink
- **Taxi / rideshare:** 10–15%
- **Hotel housekeeping:** $2–$5 per night
- **Food delivery:** 10–20% or $3–$5 minimum
