## How many countries are there in the world?

The UN recognizes 193 member states and 2 observer members (Vatican and Palestine). This game also includes several separate territories and autonomous areas, totaling over 240 flags.

## Tips for identifying flags

- **Nordic countries** share the Scandinavian cross (Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Iceland)
- **Arab countries** often use pan-Arab colors: red, white, black, and green
- **In Africa**, pan-African colors are favored: green, yellow, and red
- **Pacific island nations'** flags often feature a blue background and stars
- **Union Jack** appears in the corner of many Commonwealth countries' flags
