### Probabilities

The probability of hitting all 7 correct numbers is about **1 / 18,643,560** – in other words, a very rare event. Still, someone wins!

The formula for calculating combinations uses combination:

> **C(40,7)** = 40! / (7! × 33!) = **18,643,560**

### Do random numbers matter?

Mathematically, every number combination has exactly the same probability of winning. Statistically, previous winning numbers do not affect future draws. Our generator produces completely random numbers in the browser – no better or worse than your own choices.
