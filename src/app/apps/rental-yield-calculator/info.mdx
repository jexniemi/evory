## Rental Yield Calculator – Profitability of Property Investment

The rental yield calculator helps assess the profitability of an investment property by calculating gross and net rental yield, monthly cash flow, and payback period.

### Gross Rental Yield vs. Net Rental Yield

**Gross rental yield** is calculated without costs:

> **Gross rental yield** = (Annual rent / Property price) × 100 %

**Net rental yield** takes into account costs such as maintenance fees, insurance, and repairs:

> **Net rental yield** = ((Annual rent – Annual costs) / Property price) × 100 %

### Typical Costs

Annual costs for an investment property usually include:
- **Maintenance fee** – average 3–6 €/m²/month depending on the housing company
- **Insurance** – about 100–300 €/year
- **Vacant months** – statistically about 1–2 months per year
- **Renovations and maintenance** – rule of thumb 1–2 % of property value per year

### Taxation of Property Investment

Rental income is taxed as ordinary income in the US, with rates varying by tax bracket. Costs such as maintenance, repairs, and mortgage interest are deductible. More information: [IRS – Rental Income](https://www.irs.gov/taxtopics/tc7)
