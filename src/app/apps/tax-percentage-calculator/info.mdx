## How is tax percentage calculated?

Tax percentage or effective tax rate tells what portion of your **gross income** goes to taxes. The calculator calculates it with the formula:

**Tax percentage = paid taxes ÷ gross salary × 100**

Gross salary is the sum of net salary and paid taxes. The calculator also shows annual gross income and total taxes.

## Effective vs. marginal tax percentage

- **Effective tax percentage** tells the real tax burden relative to gross income — this is what the calculator shows
- **Marginal tax percentage** tells how much tax you pay on *additional dollar* — can be over 40% including side costs

In practice, the effective total tax rate (all withholdings included) for an average American is typically **20–35%**.

## Tips for taxation

- Check your **W-2** annually [on IRS website](https://www.irs.gov/individuals) — too high withholding ties up money unnecessarily
- Remember to claim **deductions**, **credits** and other adjustments on your tax return
- If income changes significantly mid-year, update your W-4
