## How Does the Sales Tax Calculator Work?

The sales tax calculator works in both directions — adding tax to a pre-tax price, or removing tax from a total.

**Pre-tax → Total** (add sales tax):

**Tax amount = pre-tax price × (tax rate / 100)**

**Total = pre-tax price + tax amount**

**Total → Pre-tax** (remove sales tax):

**Pre-tax price = total / (1 + tax rate / 100)**

**Tax amount = total − pre-tax price**

## US Sales Tax Overview

Sales tax in the United States is set at the state and local level — there is no federal sales tax. Rates vary significantly by state:

Five states have no state-level sales tax: **Alaska, Delaware, Montana, New Hampshire, and Oregon.**

## Who Benefits from the Sales Tax Calculator?

- **Shoppers** — find out exactly how much you'll pay at checkout
- **Business owners** — compute tax to add to invoices
- **Travelers** — understand price differences between states
- **Accountants** — verify calculations on receipts and invoices
