## Age Calculator — Calculate Your Exact Age

The age calculator determines your precise age from your date of birth to today's date. The result is displayed in multiple formats: years, months, and days, as well as totals in days, weeks, and months. Whether you need your exact age for an official form or just for fun, this tool handles leap years and varying month lengths automatically.

### How Is Age Calculated?

Age calculation compares two dates — your birth date and the current date (or any target date you choose). The algorithm works through three steps:

1. **Years** — Count full years from birthday to target date.
2. **Months** — Count remaining full months after the last birthday.
3. **Days** — Count remaining days after the last full month.

The calculator accounts for months with 28, 29, 30, or 31 days, and correctly handles February 29 for people born on leap days.

### Multiple Output Formats

The calculator shows your age in six different ways:

- **Years, months, and days** — The traditional exact age
- **Total days lived** — How many sunrises you have seen
- **Total weeks** — Useful for pregnancy tracking and fitness plans
- **Total months** — Handy for subscription and billing calculations
- **Next birthday countdown** — Days until your next birthday
- **Day of the week you were born** — A fun trivia fact

### Practical Uses of the Age Calculator

**Official and legal purposes:**
- Verifying age requirements (driver's license, voting, retirement)
- Insurance premium calculations that require exact age
- Legal age verification for contracts and consent

**Health and wellness:**
- Providing accurate age input for BMI, BMR, and calorie calculators
- Tracking developmental milestones in children
- Checking eligibility for age-specific medical screenings

**Everyday fun:**
- Finding the exact age difference between two people
- Planning milestone celebrations (10,000 days old, 1 billion seconds)
- Discovering what day of the week you were born
