## Age Calculator – Calculate Your Exact Age

The age calculator calculates your exact age from your birth date to today. The result is displayed in years, months, and days, as well as total amounts in different units.

### How is age calculated accurately?

Age is calculated by comparing the birth date to the current date. The calculator takes into account different month lengths and leap years for an accurate result.

The calculator shows your age in six different ways:
- **Years, months, and days** – traditional exact age
- **Total number of days** – how many days you've lived
- **Total number of weeks** – how many complete weeks
- **Total number of months** – how many complete months

### Where is the age calculator useful?

**Official matters:**
- Checking age limits (driver's license, voting rights, retirement age)
- Calculating insurance premiums
- Planning retirement age

**Health and wellness:**
- Age input for BMI and health calculators
- Tracking developmental stages in children
- Checking medical age limits

**In everyday life:**
- Calculating exact age difference between people
- How many days until next birthday
- Planning milestone days (e.g., 10,000 days old)

### Fun facts about age

- **10,000 days old** you are around age 27
- **1,000 weeks** is completed around age 19
- **1 billion seconds** old you are around age 31 years and 8 months
