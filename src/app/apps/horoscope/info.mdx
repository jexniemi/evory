The horoscope is based on **Western astrology**, where your sun sign is determined by your birth date. Each zodiac sign is associated with certain personality traits, an element, a ruling planet, and a modality (cardinal, fixed, or mutable).

### The Four Elements

Each sign belongs to one of four elements, which shape fundamental personality tendencies:

- **🔥 Fire** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) — Energetic, passionate, spontaneous, and action-oriented. Fire signs are natural leaders who thrive on enthusiasm and adventure.
- **🌍 Earth** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) — Practical, reliable, grounded, and methodical. Earth signs value stability, hard work, and tangible results.
- **💨 Air** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) — Intellectual, social, communicative, and idea-driven. Air signs excel at analysis, connection, and innovation.
- **💧 Water** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) — Emotionally intuitive, empathetic, and deeply feeling. Water signs navigate the world through emotion and instinct.

### Zodiac Compatibility

Astrology suggests that certain sign pairings are more naturally harmonious:

- **Same element** signs typically understand each other intuitively (e.g., two water signs)
- **Fire + Air** tend to energize each other — fire needs air to burn, and air is warmed by fire
- **Earth + Water** complement each other — water nourishes earth, and earth gives water form
- **Opposite signs** (Aries–Libra, Taurus–Scorpio, etc.) can have strong attraction but may face challenges

However, full astrological compatibility considers much more than sun signs — moon signs, rising signs, Venus placements, and the full birth chart all play roles.

### History of Western Astrology

Western astrology has its roots in **Babylonian astronomy** (around 2000 BCE), where priests tracked planetary movements to predict seasonal changes and events. The Greeks adopted and refined the system — the word "zodiac" comes from the Greek *zōidiakos kyklos* ("circle of animals").

The **twelve-sign zodiac** as we know it was established around the 5th century BCE. During the Renaissance and Enlightenment, astrology gradually separated from astronomy as science advanced. Today, about **29% of Americans** believe in astrology according to Pew Research Center surveys, with interest growing especially among younger adults.

### Sun Sign vs. Full Birth Chart

Your **sun sign** is determined solely by your birth date and represents your core identity and ego. However, a complete astrological profile includes:

- **Moon sign** — Your emotional nature and inner world
- **Rising (Ascendant) sign** — How others perceive you; determined by the exact time and location of birth
- **Venus sign** — Love style and what you value
- **Mars sign** — Drive, ambition, and how you pursue goals
- **Mercury sign** — Communication style

### Daily Number

The daily number shown in the horoscope is calculated from the current date and your sign. It changes daily and is intended purely as entertainment — not as a scientific prediction or financial advice.
