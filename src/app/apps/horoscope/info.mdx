### Elements and Their Meaning

Each sign belongs to one of four elements:

- **Fire** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) – Energetic, passionate, impulsive
- **Earth** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) – Practical, reliable, systematic
- **Air** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) – Intellectual, social, communicative
- **Water** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) – Emotionally sensitive, intuitive, empathetic

### Compatibility

Generally, signs of the same element get along best. Fire and air support each other, as do earth and water. However, this is only a general guideline – real compatibility is much more complex.

### Daily Number

The daily number is calculated based on the date and your sign and changes daily. It's fun entertainment value, not a scientific prediction.
