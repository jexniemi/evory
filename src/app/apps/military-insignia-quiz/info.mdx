## Finnish Army Military Ranks

The Finnish Defense Forces' Army has 21 military ranks, divided into enlisted, NCOs, cadet officers and officers. Insignia are attached to shoulders and their shape indicates the rank order.

## How do insignia differ?

- **Enlisted and NCOs** use chevrons — the more stripes, the higher the rank
- **Officers'** insignia have rosettes (stars) and metal shoulder boards
- **Generals'** insignia have lions and gold-colored shoulder boards

## Tips for the game

Start by learning the extremes: private (empty) and general (lion + sword). After that, you learn to distinguish the intermediate ranks more easily.
