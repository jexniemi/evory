### Common Speed References

- **Walking** – ~3 mph (~5 km/h)
- **Jogging** – ~6 mph (~10 km/h)
- **Cycling** – ~12–19 mph (20–30 km/h)
- **Urban speed limit** – 31 mph (50 km/h)
- **Highway** – 62 mph (100 km/h / 54 kn)
- **Airplane** – ~560 mph (~900 km/h / 486 kn)

### Knots – A Navigator's Unit

The knot is the standard speed unit for ships and aircraft. One knot equals one nautical mile per hour (1.852 km/h). It is internationally used in maritime navigation and aviation.
