## Apartment Price Per Square Foot – What Does It Mean and How Is It Calculated?

Price per square foot, or price per square meter, is one of the most important metrics in apartment sales. It allows comparison of prices of apartments of different sizes in the same area and helps assess if the apartment is priced at market value.

### How Is Price Per Square Foot Calculated?

Price per square foot is calculated by dividing the apartment's debt-free total price by its area:

**Price per square foot = Total cost ÷ Area (sq ft)**

Total cost means the purchase price plus any immediate renovation or repair expenses needed to make the property livable.

### Why Does Total Cost Matter?

A home's true cost includes the purchase price plus any repairs or renovations needed. Two homes listed at different prices can have the same real cost:

### Factors Affecting Price Per Square Foot

- **Location** – neighborhood, services, transport connections
- **Condition** – renovation needs lower price per square foot
- **Housing company's condition** – upcoming plumbing renovations and facade renovations
- **Year built** – new builds more expensive
- **Floor and views** – higher floors usually more expensive
- **Apartment size** – small apartments often more expensive per square foot

### Tips for Apartment Buyers

1. Always compare **debt-free** price per square foot
2. Check the area's average price per square foot from the [Zillow](https://www.zillow.com/) service
3. Consider the housing company's upcoming renovations – they can significantly increase the real price
4. Request the property manager's certificate before the purchase
