## Calorie Burn Calculator – Calculate exercise calorie expenditure

The calorie burn calculator calculates an estimate of the calorie expenditure of a physical performance using MET values (Metabolic Equivalent of Task). MET tells the energy consumption of the activity relative to rest.

### How do MET values work?

MET value 1 corresponds to sitting at rest (about 1 kcal/kg/h). The calorie consumption of the activity is calculated with the formula:

> **Calories (kcal)** = MET × weight (kg) × time (h)

Example: 75 kg person runs 30 min (MET 10):
> 10 × 75 × 0.5 h = **375 kcal**

### Notes on calorie calculation

MET values are averages and the calculator does not take into account gender, age, fitness level or exercise efficiency. Actual values may deviate 10–30%. The calculator is suitable for indicative estimation.

Calorie calculation is based on Ainsworth et al. (2011) *Compendium of Physical Activities* research. Explore the original database: [Compendium of Physical Activities](https://sites.google.com/site/compendiumofphysicalactivities/)
