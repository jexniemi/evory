## How to calculate your hourly wage

Hourly wage is calculated by dividing the monthly salary by the monthly working hours:

**Hourly wage = Monthly salary ÷ (Weekly hours × 52 ÷ 12)**

For example, with a gross monthly salary of €3,200 and 37.5 h weekly working hours, the hourly wage is approximately **€19.69/h**.

### Gross or net?

The calculator works with both gross and net salary. Gross salary is the salary before taxes and side costs. Net salary is the take-home amount. When comparing salaries, e.g. in job search, always use gross salary — that's the figure stated in job advertisements and employment contracts.

### When is knowing your hourly wage useful?

- **In salary negotiations** — easier to compare different offers when you know the real hourly rate
- **Checking overtime compensation** — overtime pay is calculated based on hourly wage (50% or 100% increase)
- **Freelancer pricing** — if you switch from salaried work to entrepreneurship, your hourly wage gives a basis for pricing (remember to add side costs on top)
- **Comparing part-time work** — is 20 h/week part-time really more profitable than full-time?
