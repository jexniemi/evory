## Work Hours Calculator – Calculate daily work hours and earnings

The work hours calculator calculates how many hours you have worked in a day and optionally your daily earnings. Enter your start and end times and breaks, and you'll get an accurate calculation.

### Why calculate work hours?

- **Time reporting** – Time sheets to send to clients or employers
- **Overtime tracking** – How many overtime hours did you get today?
- **Freelancer billing** – Calculate billable hours per client
- **Payroll** – Check your daily gross income from hourly wage

### Calculating work hours

The time worked is calculated simply:

> **Time worked** = End time – Start time – Breaks

If you work a night shift (for example, 10:00 PM–6:00 AM), activate the "Night shift" setting. This way, the calculator handles shifts that go past midnight correctly.

### Overtime

In the US, regular working time is up to 8 hours per day and 40 hours per week. For hours exceeding this, overtime compensation must be paid:
- First 2 overtime hours: +50% to wage
- Thereafter: +100% to wage
