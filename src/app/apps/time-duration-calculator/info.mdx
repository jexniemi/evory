## Time Duration Calculator – Find Hours and Minutes Between Times

Need to know how long something takes? This time duration calculator finds the difference between two times, or adds and subtracts hours and minutes from any starting time. It shows results in hours and minutes, total minutes, and decimal hours — perfect for timesheets, scheduling, and payroll calculations.

## How to Calculate Time Duration

To find the duration between two times:

> **Duration = End Time − Start Time**
>
> If the result is negative, add 24 hours (the end time is the next day).

**Example**: From 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM = 8 hours 30 minutes = 8.5 decimal hours = 510 total minutes.

## Practical Uses

- **Timesheets**: Calculate daily and weekly work hours for payroll
- **Scheduling**: Determine meeting durations and break times
- **Travel planning**: Figure out arrival times when adding flight/drive duration
- **Cooking**: Add prep time and cooking time together
- **Project management**: Track time spent on tasks
- **Overtime**: Calculate hours beyond the standard work day

## Tips for Time Tracking

- **Round to the nearest quarter hour** if your employer uses 15-minute increments (common for payroll)
- **Subtract breaks**: Remember to deduct lunch and other break times from total hours
- **Use 24-hour format** to avoid AM/PM confusion in calculations
- **Track consistently**: Enter time as you work rather than trying to remember later
- **Decimal hours for payroll**: Many payroll systems use decimal hours (7.75 instead of 7:45)
