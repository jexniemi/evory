### Use Cases

- **Real Estate** – Sizes of American homes are often stated in square feet (ft²). 1,000 ft² ≈ 93 m²
- **Agriculture** – Field area is stated in hectares in many countries, in acres in the United States. 1 acre ≈ 0.4 ha
- **Plot Size** – Housing fairs and real estate trade use m² or ha

### Hectare and Acre

Hectare (10,000 m²) is a common unit in agriculture and forestry. One acre is about 0.405 hectares — almost as large as an American football field.
