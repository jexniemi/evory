## What does percentage mean?

Percentage (%) literally means "per hundred" (lat. *per centum*). It is a way to express a share or ratio as part of a hundred:

- 100 % = the whole amount
- 50 % = half
- 25 % = quarter
- 1 % = one hundredth

## Three functions of the percentage calculator

### 1. How much is X% of Y?

With this calculator, you find the concrete value when you know the percentage and the total amount.

**Formula:** result = (percentage ÷ 100) × value

**Example:** 15% discount on a 200 € product: 15 ÷ 100 × 200 = **30 €**

### 2. What percentage is X of Y?

With this, you find out what share a certain value is of the whole.

**Formula:** percentage = (part ÷ whole) × 100

**Example:** 35 students out of 200 got grade 5: 35 ÷ 200 × 100 = **17.5 %**

### 3. Percentage change

Percentage change tells how much a value has changed relative to the initial value.

**Formula:** change-% = ((end value − start value) ÷ |start value|) × 100

**Example:** Salary increased from 2,800 € to 3,200 €: (400 ÷ 2,800) × 100 = **+14.3 %**

## Tips for percentage calculations

- **Percentage is easy to calculate in your head:** 10 % = divide by ten, 5 % = divide by twenty, 20 % = fifth
- **Mnemonic:** "Percentage" × "Whole" ÷ 100 = value
- Percentage change **is not symmetric**: +50 % and then −50 % does not return to the original value (but stays at 75 %)
