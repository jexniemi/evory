## Pregnancy Calculator – Calculate Due Date and Pregnancy Weeks

The pregnancy calculator calculates the due date, current pregnancy week, and trimester based on the first day of the last menstrual period (LMP). The calculator also shows important ultrasound dates and other milestones.

### How are pregnancy weeks calculated?

Pregnancy is calculated from the **first day of the last menstrual period (LMP)**, not from conception. Therefore, pregnancy weeks include the time before conception. The due date is **40 weeks (280 days)** from LMP.

### Important Ultrasounds

- **Dating ultrasound (weeks 11–14)** – Determines fetal size, due date, and nuchal translucency (screening for chromosomal abnormalities)
- **Anatomy ultrasound (weeks 18–21)** – Checks fetal structures and organ development
- **Additional ultrasounds** as needed later in pregnancy

### Due Date

The due date is an estimate, not an exact day. Full term is considered weeks 37–42. Only about 5% of babies are born on the exact due date.
