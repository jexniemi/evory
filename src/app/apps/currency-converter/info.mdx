### Important note on rates

The calculator's rates are **indicative** and based on approximate reference rates. Actual exchange rates vary daily and the bank or exchange point charges its own margin.

You can find up-to-date rates at:
- [ECB – Reference Rates](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/stats/policy_and_exchange_rates/euro_reference_exchange_rates/)
