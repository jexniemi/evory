## Electricity Bill Calculator

The electricity bill calculator helps you estimate your monthly or annual bill.

**Formula:**

**Electricity bill = consumption (kWh) × unit price (€/kWh)**

Note that the bill may include additional charges such as taxes, transmission fees, and basic fees.
