## Whose Name Day is Today?

The name day search tool automatically shows today's name day names and allows you to search for any Finnish name's name day or browse the calendar by date. The service is based on the official Finnish name day calendar.

## What are Finnish name days based on?

The Finnish name day calendar is a list maintained by the Almanac Office, where one or more names are assigned to each day. The calendar contains over 700 names, and it is updated occasionally as naming trends change. Traditionally, name days were based on Catholic saints' memorial days, but the current Finnish calendar is independent and secular.

## Most Popular Finnish Name Days

Certain name days are celebrated particularly widely – for example, Jussi (January), Eetu (February), Mikael (September), and Juhani (June) are high in popularity. What they have in common is the popularity of the names in Finland.

## How are name days celebrated in Finland?

Name days have traditionally been celebrated almost as importantly as birthdays. The custom is to congratulate, send a card or message, and offer coffee with cake. In many workplaces, name days are still remembered today. With social media, congratulatory messages have largely moved online.

## Name Days and Naming

In Finland, the name day tradition is often related to naming: a child may be given a name partly based on when they were born or because parents have a certain name day in mind. The first name is registered in the population information system and must be approved for Finnish naming practices.
