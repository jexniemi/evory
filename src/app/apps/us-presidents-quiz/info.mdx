## U.S. Presidents Quiz

This U.S. Presidents Quiz turns American history into a fast challenge. You get a clue about a presidency or historical event and pick the correct president from four choices.

### Why this quiz is fun and educational

- Quick rounds with immediate feedback
- Mix of famous events and presidency facts
- Great for classrooms, trivia nights, and personal study
- Streak, score, and level mechanics keep it addictive

### How to play

1. Read the clue carefully.
2. Choose one president from four options.
3. Correct answers build combo and points.
4. Win before your 3 lives are gone.
