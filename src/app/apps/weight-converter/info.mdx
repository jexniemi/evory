### Quick Conversion Formulas

> **Pounds → Kilograms:** Multiply by 0.4536 (or divide by 2.205)
>
> **Kilograms → Pounds:** Multiply by 2.2046
>
> **Ounces → Grams:** Multiply by 28.35
>
> **Grams → Ounces:** Multiply by 0.03527

### Worked Example

Convert **175 pounds** to kilograms:

175 × 0.4536 = **79.4 kg**

Convert **5 kg 250 g** of flour to pounds and ounces:

5.25 kg × 2.2046 = 11.57 lbs → **11 lbs 9 oz**

### US Ton vs. Metric Ton

Americans use the **short ton** (2,000 lbs / 907 kg), while most of the world uses the **metric ton** or "tonne" (2,204.6 lbs / 1,000 kg). The difference is about 10%, which matters significantly in shipping, commodities trading, and industrial applications. The UK historically used the **long ton** (2,240 lbs / 1,016 kg), though it has largely been replaced by the metric ton.

### Mass vs. Weight — The Technical Difference

In everyday conversation, "weight" and "mass" are interchangeable. Scientifically, however, **mass** measures the amount of matter (constant everywhere) while **weight** is the force of gravity on that mass (varies by location). A 150 lb person on Earth would weigh only 25 lbs on the Moon but would still have the same mass.
