### Common Conversions

- **1 pound (lb) = 0.453 kg** – Used in the US and UK
- **1 stone = 14 lbs = 6.35 kg** – Common in the UK and Ireland for body weight
- **1 metric ton = 1,000 kg** – Standard in industry and shipping
- **70 kg ≈ 154 lbs ≈ 11 st** – Typical adult body weight

### When Is a Weight Converter Useful?

- **Travel** – Convert between metric and imperial weights
- **Sports** – Compare competition weight categories
- **Cooking** – Convert recipe measurements between US and metric units
- **Online shopping** – Check product weights from international stores before buying

### Quick Conversion Tips

To convert **pounds → kilograms**: multiply by 0.454.  
To convert **kilograms → pounds**: multiply by 2.205.
