## Macro Calculator – Calculate daily calories and macronutrients

The macro calculator calculates your daily calorie needs and appropriate macronutrient ratios – protein, carbohydrates and fat – based on your body's weight, goal and activity level.

### What are macronutrients?

Macronutrients (or "macros") are nutrients from which the body gets energy:

- **Protein** – 4 kcal/g. Needed for muscle building and maintenance. Important for everyone.
- **Carbohydrates** – 4 kcal/g. The body's and brain's primary energy source.
- **Fat** – 9 kcal/g. Needed for hormone function, vitamin absorption and cell building.

### How does calorie calculation work?

The calculator uses a simplified BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) estimate, which is multiplied by an activity factor. Depending on your goal, calories are added or subtracted:

### Activity levels

- **Sedentary work** – No exercise or very little (factor 1.2)
- **Light exercise** – 1–3 days per week (factor 1.375)
- **Moderate exercise** – 3–5 days per week (factor 1.55)
- **Intense exercise** – 6–7 days per week (factor 1.725)

### Important note

The calculator gives an indicative estimate. Individual differences in metabolism, age, gender and body composition affect actual needs. For more accurate guidance, consult a nutritionist.
