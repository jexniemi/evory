## Portion Calculator – Scale Recipe to the Right Serving Size

The portion calculator changes the number of servings in a recipe to your desired amount. Enter the original ingredients and serving size, select the desired serving size – the calculator scales all amounts automatically.

### When is a portion calculator useful?

- **Halving a recipe** – The cooking recipe is for 4, but you're cooking for only 2
- **Increasing a recipe** – You want to make a cake for 20 people instead of the original 8
- **Party cooking** – Scale the recipe for 50 people for parties
- **Diet tracking** – Calculate exact gram amounts for a specific serving size

### Scaling Factor

The scaling factor is calculated simply:

> **Factor** = Desired serving size / Original serving size

For example, if the original recipe is for 4 and you want to make it for 10, the factor is 2.5 and all ingredients are multiplied by 2.5.

### Tips

- Note that **baking time does not always scale directly** with the amount – two cakes bake almost as fast as one
- **For spices**, scaling usually works well, but check according to taste
- **Baking powder and yeast** – In large quantities, the ratio may be slightly smaller than direct scaling
