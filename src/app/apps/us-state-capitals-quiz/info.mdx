## U.S. State Capitals Quiz

The U.S. State Capitals Quiz helps you learn all 50 state capitals in a fast and fun way. You’ll see a state name and choose the correct capital from four options. Every correct answer builds your streak and increases your points.

### Why this quiz is useful

Knowing state capitals is helpful for students, teachers, trivia fans, and anyone preparing for civics or geography tests. Repetition with immediate feedback is one of the best ways to memorize geography facts.

### How to play

1. Read the state shown on screen.
2. Select one of four capital city options.
3. Keep your streak alive for higher score and faster leveling.
4. Reach the win target before you run out of lives.
